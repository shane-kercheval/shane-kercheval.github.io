Most Startups Fail.

Starting a startup is difficult. You might have a great idea, but identifying all the possible risks and challenges is either overlooked, or overwhelming. I help entrepreneurs build a strategy that decreases risk and uncovers opportunities.

  • Strategy Formation

    This package is for first-time founders who want to figure out the best way to move forward. Together, we’ll come up with a strategy that explains how to move forward and overcome the inherent challenges, and we’ll develop a framework for decision making that reduces risk.

  • OnGoing Consulting

    This package is for first-time founders who want continual expertise and feedback while growing their business. You will get ongoing strategic advice and insight from someone who will become part of your team. Together, we’ll move faster and overcome challenges.

  • Financial Forecasting

    This package is for first-time founders who need to develop a financial forecast model for new business ideas. A lot of startups fail because they simply don’t forecast correctly. Together, we’ll come up with a realistic forecast model that allows you to plan for success.
